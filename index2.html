Get to know the co-founders

Created by three Audencia business school students, Tirolin'air was launched this year with its first 6 stations based in Paris. Revolutionising the way tourists discover a city, but also rediscovering it for Parisians, this project is already a great success.

Maxime

One of the firm co-founder, Maxime has been chosen to become chief executive officer of Tirolin'air. He is responsible for coordinating operations, managing relations with partners and ensuring that the company is well organised.

Juliette

One of the firm co-founder, Juliette is the marketing director of Tirolin'air. She is responsible for managing the company's communication operations, but also for managing the social networks and the website.

Long

One of the firm co-founder, Long is the financial director of Tirolin'air. He looks after the company's accounts and is constantly on the lookout for new partners and investors.

Get to know our CSR strategy

Aware of the current ecological and social difficulties, our project seeks to provide a solution to some of these issues.

  • From an ecological point of view : Less traffic congestion & less emissions

  • From a sanitary point of view : People pass one by one, thus avoiding contagion and relieving the congestion of conventional public transport

  • From a social point of view : Possibility of employing people with disabilities, in social reintegration or without a diploma.
